# Pan‑African Integrated Digital Marketplace (PAIDM)

## 1. Vision & Objectives

Create a **single, secure, Pan‑African digital marketplace** that connects **Businesses, Traders, Manufacturers, and Consumers** across African countries based on **geographical location**, enabling:

* Cross‑border and local trade
* Drop‑shipping to the nearest fulfillment point
* Secure payments via **Mobile Money & Credit Cards**
* In‑app communication & trust systems
* Paid advertising for businesses

The platform will function similarly to **Alibaba**, but optimized for **African infrastructure, payments, logistics, and regulations**.

---

## 2. Core User Types & Roles

### 2.1 User Categories

1. **Consumers** – Buy goods & services
2. **Traders / Resellers** – Buy wholesale, sell retail
3. **Manufacturers** – Produce and sell at scale
4. **Logistics Partners** – Fulfillment & delivery
5. **Advertisers** – Businesses promoting products
6. **Administrators** – Platform governance & trust

Each user can **upgrade roles** without creating a new account.

---

## 3. Account Creation & Identity Management

### 3.1 Registration

* Email + Password
* Phone Number (Mandatory)
* Country & City (Geo‑binding)
* Optional National ID / Business Registration (for trust tiers)

### 3.2 Authentication

* Email verification
* OTP via SMS / WhatsApp
* Optional 2FA

### 3.3 Business Verification Levels

| Level    | Requirements   | Benefits           |
| -------- | -------------- | ------------------ |
| Basic    | Email + Phone  | Limited listings   |
| Verified | Business Docs  | Higher trust badge |
| Trusted  | Escrow history | Featured ranking   |

## 4. Marketplace Architecture

### 4.1 Product Listings

* Product name, category, SKU
* Photos & videos
* Pricing (local & USD equivalent)
* MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity)
* Available locations
* Shipping options

### 4.2 Geo‑Based Discovery

* Country → City → Radius filtering
* Auto‑recommend **nearest supplier**
* Local language & currency display

### 4.3 Wholesale & Retail Support

* Tiered pricing
* Bulk order discounts
* Dropshipping enabled

---

## 5. Drop‑Shipping & Logistics Engine

### 5.1 Smart Fulfillment Logic

1. Buyer places order
2. System checks:

   * Nearest stock
   * Fastest delivery
   * Lowest cost
3. Assigns logistics partner automatically

### 5.2 Logistics Integration

* Local courier APIs
* Cross‑border shipping providers
* Real‑time tracking
* Proof of delivery (Photo + OTP)

### 5.3 Warehousing (Phase 2)

* Regional hubs (East, West, Southern Africa)
* Inventory pooling for fast delivery
