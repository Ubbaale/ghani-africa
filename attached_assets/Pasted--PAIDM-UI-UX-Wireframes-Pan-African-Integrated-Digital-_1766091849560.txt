# PAIDM – UI/UX Wireframes

*(Pan-African Integrated Digital Marketplace)*

> **Note:** These are **platform-ready wireframes** describing layout, components, and user flows. They can be handed directly to **UI designers (Figma/Adobe XD)** or **frontend engineers**.

---

## 1. Global Design Principles

* **Mobile-first** (Android primary)
* Low-bandwidth friendly
* Clear trust indicators
* Location-aware by default
* Multi-language ready

### Color & Visual Language

* Primary: Deep Green (Trade & Growth)
* Secondary: Gold / Amber (Trust & Value)
* Neutral: White, Light Grey
* Status: Red (Alerts), Blue (Info)

### Core Navigation Pattern

* Bottom Tab Bar (Mobile)
* Persistent Search
* Floating “Post Item” button (Business users)
